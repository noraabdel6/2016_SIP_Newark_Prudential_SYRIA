Our Vision

This is a nonprofit created to empower, educate, and engage Syrian refugees living in the US who are currently looking to pursue a college education.


Our goal is to guide refugees through the complicated college application process by pairing them with experienced college students. Through this mentorship process, students will be able to find resources and seek guidance in other aspects of their lives.

iCan is dedicated to aiding Syrian refugees in the college application process and giving them the opportunities they need to achieve higher-level education.
